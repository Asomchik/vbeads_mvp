SHIPPING

Beads are shipped from the California, USA*
Shipping & handling costs are calculated according to a formula (because of a composite shipping route from Uruguay to you):
Each bead multiplies $2 plus:
-Within the US: + $5 for 1-3 beads, + $10 starting from 4 beads (priority mail).
-Europe: +$20,
-Canada: +$16,
-Australia: +$22
(So three beads to Canada: $22=3x2+16, or five beads within US: $20=5x2+10)
All packages have a tracking number. You will receive a notification with tracking details as soon as items have been shipped. Parcels can only be shipped to the address stated in your PayPal.

*In rare cases the beads may be shipped from another location (Cyprus). In this case, you will see it on a product page.
